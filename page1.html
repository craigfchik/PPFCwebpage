About The Pool Man Pvt Ltd t/a Pool Pump

Out of a partnership from Pool Pump and Filter Centre (PPFC), the Pool Man Pvt Ltd was born. The Pool Man Pvt Ltd which operates under the trade name Pool Pump (PP) is the visionary work of a great Director who at first thought of PPFC which he later acquired a new strategic partner to start out as a new entity PP. It is still the good old PPFC operating under a new name and another new alliance, for your convenience. Pool Man Pvt Ltd t/a Pool Pump will still prides itself in the swimming pool industry with more than 15years operating making it a name to reckon with. It remains the most customer sensitive swimming pool company in Zimbabwe by far. Pool Pump understands that an investing in swimming pools is worth it, quality and professionalism being the lead factors and motivators in converting your vision into a breath taking pool shape and design. Pool Pump is also the sole distributor or Speck brand products in the country. In the pool industry, PPFC is renowned for professional workmanship and inspired by good governance.
